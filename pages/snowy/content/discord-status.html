My Discord Status Meanings

As I am a admin for a Discord server, I thought I'd create this.

Yeah, the server is QUITE dead, but I care for it with my heart <3

General Status Types

  • Online – Available, might be busy or not... I might respond quickly or take a couple of minutes with this status. It depends.
  • Idle / Away – Away from my device. Most likely available or busy.
  • Do Not Disturb / DND – Busy, available, or just don’t want to be disturbed. It’s fine to ping or DM me about something. I may take some time to respond in this status.
  • Offline / Invisible – Hiding from the world. Likely busy or available. It’s fine to ping or DM me about something.

Custom Status Meanings (Emojis)

  • 🌙 – Tired. Best not to disturb. It's fine to check up on me and/or warn me about something in DMs.
  • 💻 – Working on something. I’m slightly busy but still able to talk if needed.
  • ⚠️ – Busy or stressed. Better not to disturb. Best to check up on me and give any warnings in DMs.
  • 💤 – Probably dead (not literally — just sleeping).
  • 🚫 – Not social. I'd prefer silence. You can still ping or DM me, but I may be angy.
  • 💬 – Free to talk!
  • 🔇 – Muted in VC... OR I’m lurking. Who knows 🤷‍♂️
  • 💢 – Annoyed or frustrated. Fine to contact, but do so with caution.
